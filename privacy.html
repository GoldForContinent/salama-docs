Your Privacy Matters to Us

At Salama Docs, we're committed to protecting your personal information while providing a seamless document recovery service. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our document recovery services, we collect various types of information:

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use Salama Docs services, you may provide:

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, phone number, physical address
  • Document Details: Type of lost document (ID, passport, etc.), document numbers, issue dates
  • Identity Verification: Scans or photos of identification documents (only when necessary for verification)
  • Account Information: Username, password (hashed), security questions
  • Payment Information: Mobile money details or credit card information (processed securely by our payment partners)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with our platform:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP or precise location (with your permission when reporting found documents)
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies, authentication tokens, analytics data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our document recovery services:

Purpose Information Used Legal Basis
Document recovery services Contact info, document details Contractual necessity
Identity verification ID scans, personal details Legal obligation
Reward distribution Contact info, payment details Contractual necessity
Service improvement Usage data, feedback Legitimate interest
Fraud prevention Device info, usage patterns Legitimate interest

2.1 Core Service Functions

We primarily use your data to:

  • Match lost documents with found reports
  • Verify your identity and document ownership
  • Notify you when your document is found
  • Facilitate secure document return
  • Process rewards for document finders

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information in these limited circumstances:

3.1 With Government Authorities

As required by Kenyan law, we share document information with:

  • National Registration Bureau for national IDs
  • Department of Immigration for passports
  • Local administration offices (chiefs' offices)

3.2 With Document Finders

When your document is found, we share:

  • Basic document type (without sensitive numbers)
  • Your contact method preference (SMS, email, or both)
  • No direct contact details until you authorize release

3.3 Service Providers

We work with trusted partners who assist our operations:

  • Payment Processors: For secure reward transactions
  • Cloud Hosting: AWS Africa (Johannesburg) for data storage
  • SMS Gateways: For document status notifications
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics (anonymized data only)

4. Document Verification Process

To prevent fraud and ensure legitimate claims, we verify documents through:

4.1 Automated Checks

  • Document number validation against official patterns
  • Cross-checking reported loss with found reports
  • Basic image analysis to detect obvious forgeries

4.2 Manual Verification

For certain cases, our team manually verifies:

  • High-value documents (passports, professional licenses)
  • Documents with potential fraudulent flags
  • Cases requiring chief's office confirmation

Verification Security

All document scans are encrypted and stored securely. Verification staff only see redacted versions of sensitive information during the process.

5. Reward System Data Handling

Our document finder reward system processes:

5.1 Finder Information

  • Mobile number for airtime rewards
  • MPESA details for cash rewards (optional)
  • Document handover confirmation details

5.2 Reward Processing

When a document is successfully returned:

  1. System verifies document receipt at chief's office
  2. Confirms owner has collected the document
  3. Processes reward to finder within 3 business days
  4. Sends confirmation to both parties

Reward Transparency

Finders can track reward status in their dashboard. All rewards are subject to verification and may be withheld if fraud is suspected.

6. Data Security Measures

We implement robust security to protect your information:

6.1 Technical Protections

  • Encryption: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, DDoS protection
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments

6.2 Document Handling

Special protections for sensitive documents:

  • Automatic redaction of sensitive numbers in storage
  • Watermarking of all document images
  • Automatic deletion of unnecessary scans after verification

6.3 Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

Data Type Retention Period
Account information 3 years after last activity
Document recovery records 5 years
Payment records 7 years (tax compliance)
Document scans 90 days after verification (unless required for investigation)

7. Your Data Rights

Under Kenyan data protection laws, you have rights to:

Access Your Data

Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you in a structured format.

Correct Inaccuracies

Update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Request Deletion

Ask us to delete your personal data when no longer needed for our services.

Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

Data Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format for transfer to another service.

Lodge Complaints

File complaints with Kenya's Office of the Data Protection Commissioner if concerned about our data practices.

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@salamadocs.co.ke. We respond within 30 days and may request verification of your identity.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

8.1 Notification Process

For significant changes, we will:

  • Post a notice on our website 30 days before changes take effect
  • Send email notifications to active users
  • Require acceptance of updated terms for continued service use
