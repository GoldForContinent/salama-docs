Common Questions

Reporting Lost Documents

How do I report a lost document?

To report a lost document, simply log in to your Salama Docs account and navigate to the "Report Lost Document" section. Fill in the required details about your lost document, including the type of document, last known location, and any identifying information. Once submitted, our system will immediately begin searching our database and alerting our network of partners.

What information do I need to provide when reporting?

When reporting a lost document, you'll need to provide:

  • Type of document (ID, passport, etc.)
  • Document number (if known)
  • Your full name as it appears on the document
  • Approximate date and location where it was lost
  • Any distinguishing features (special endorsements, stickers, etc.)

The more details you provide, the better our chances of recovering your document.

Is there a fee to report a lost document?

No, reporting a lost document is completely free. You only pay a small recovery fee if we successfully locate and return your document to you. This fee varies depending on the document type and delivery method you choose.

Document Recovery Process

How does Salama Docs recover lost documents?

We use a multi-pronged approach to recover lost documents:

  1. Database Matching: We check all newly registered found documents against our database of reported losses.
  2. Partner Network: We work with police stations, transport companies, hotels, and other institutions where documents are commonly found.
  3. Community Alerts: When appropriate, we alert our network of trusted community members in the area where your document was lost.
  4. Verification: All potential matches undergo rigorous verification to ensure we're returning documents to their rightful owners.
How long does recovery usually take?

Recovery times vary depending on several factors:

  • Document Type: IDs are typically recovered faster than passports or academic certificates.
  • Location: Documents lost in urban areas with more of our partners tend to be recovered faster.
  • Reporting Time: The sooner you report after losing your document, the better your chances of quick recovery.

On average, 65% of documents are recovered within 72 hours, and 85% within one week.

How will I know if my document is found?

When your document is found and verified, you'll receive:

  • An immediate SMS notification to your registered phone number
  • An email with detailed instructions for recovery
  • A notification in your Salama Docs account dashboard
  • Follow-up reminders if you don't respond within 24 hours

You can also check your recovery status anytime by logging into your account.

Security & Privacy

Is my personal information secure with Salama Docs?

Absolutely. We take your privacy and security very seriously:

  • All data is encrypted using military-grade AES-256 encryption
  • We never share your full personal details with finders
  • Document verification is done through secure, government-approved methods
  • Our systems undergo regular security audits by independent firms

You can review our full Privacy Policy for more details.

How do you verify document ownership?

We use multiple verification methods to ensure documents are returned to their rightful owners:

  • Biometric Matching: For IDs and passports, we verify against government databases when possible.
  • Security Questions: We ask questions only the true owner would know about the document.
  • Document Features: We verify unique features like signatures, stamps, or markings.
  • Secondary Verification: In some cases, we may request additional documentation.

This rigorous process ensures no document is ever returned to the wrong person.

Delivery & Collection

How can I get my document back once it's found?

We offer several convenient options for document recovery:

  1. Pickup Centers: Collect from one of our 47 verified recovery centers nationwide.
  2. Secure Delivery: Have your document delivered to your home or office (additional fee applies).
  3. Agent Collection: Authorize a trusted person to collect on your behalf with proper verification.
  4. Partner Locations: Some documents can be collected from our partner institutions like police stations.

You'll be able to choose your preferred method when we notify you that your document has been found.

What are your delivery fees and timelines?

Our delivery options and fees are as follows:

Service Fee Timeline
Standard Delivery KSh 300 2-3 business days
Express Delivery KSh 600 24 hours (Nairobi), 48 hours (other cities)
Self Collection Free Immediate upon verification

Fees are payable upon confirmation of delivery method.

What if I'm not in Kenya when my document is found?

We have special procedures for international recovery:

  • Documents can be held securely at our Nairobi headquarters for up to 90 days
  • We offer international courier services (DHL, FedEx) at competitive rates
  • You can authorize someone to collect on your behalf with proper documentation
  • For passports, we can coordinate with embassies when appropriate

Contact our support team for personalized assistance with international recoveries.
