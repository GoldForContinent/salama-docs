Frequently Asked Questions

We use a multi-step verification process including security question verification, and in-person ID confirmation at our partner Huduma Centers.

Basic recovery services are completely free for citizens. Optional expedited services may carry a small fee for processing.

84% of documents are returned within 72 hours when found in major urban areas. Rural areas may take 3-5 business days depending on location.
