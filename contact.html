Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our document recovery services

How do I report a lost document?

You can report a lost document by logging into your account and filling out the lost document form. If you don't have an account yet, you can sign up first. The process takes less than 2 minutes and you'll receive a confirmation with your tracking number.

What should I do if I find someone's ID?

If you find any identification document, you can bring it to any of our recovery centers or register it on our website. We'll notify the owner immediately while keeping your information confidential. You can also drop it at any police station.

How long does recovery typically take?

Our average recovery time is 24 hours, with 98% of documents being recovered within this timeframe. However, recovery times can vary depending on where the document was lost and how quickly it's found by someone.

Is there a fee for document recovery?

Basic recovery services are free of charge. However, if you require express delivery or special handling, there may be a small fee. Any potential costs will always be communicated to you upfront before proceeding.

How do I know my personal information is safe?

We use military-grade encryption to protect all your data. Finders never see your personal contact information - all communication is handled through our secure platform. We also comply with all Kenyan data protection regulations.
